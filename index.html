Meal Planning

Main Course

A 3 day meal plan that satisfies nutritional requirments for breakfast, lunch, and dinner

Day 1.

Breakfast: Egg Ommlete

it is high in lean protein, and is a tasty meal to start off the meal plan.



Lunch: Salad

salads are high in vitamins, and with lean meat included, can be a source of protein too.



Dinner: Spaghetti

spaghetti can be a good source of some fiber and mostly complex carbohydrates.

Day 2.

Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait

provides a good source of protein, fiber, and calcium.



Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with quinoa

good source of protein and fiber



Dinner: Baked salmon with roasted vegetables

high in protein, and has a considerable amount of fiber too

Day 3.

Breakfast: Oatmeal with fruits and nuts

it is a good source of fiber, protein, and complex carbohydrates.



Lunch: Vegetable stir-fry

good source of protein, and complex carbohydrates.



Dinner: Chickpea and spinach curry with rice

it is high in fiber and protein
